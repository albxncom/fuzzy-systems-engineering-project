Course Finder

This application can be used to find a selection of courses of the JMCS that match the desired criterias. The results are computed by fuzzy etc..

General Questions

1. To which track should the courses belong to?

Explanation
Every course belongs to one or more tracks, specified at https://mcs.unibnf.ch.

2. Are you ok with mandatory exercises?
3. What languages would you prefer?

Questions concerning the language

1. How important is the language of the lectures for you?
Explanation: This includes how good the lecturers speak the language in which they holds the course as well as the learning material.
2. How important is the language of the exercises for you?
Explanation: This includes how good the assistants speak the language in which they hold the exercise class as well as the exercise material.

Questions concering the contents of the course

1. How important is it for you that the course is understandable?
Explanation: This includes that the contents are conveyed in an understandable way and the material is illustrated with examples.
2. How difficult do you want the lectures and exercices to be?
Explanation: This includes how difficult it is to follow the exercise classes as well as how difficult the exercises are.
3. How important is the commitment of the lecturers and assistants?
Explanation: This includes how well the lectureres and assistants listen to students' inputs and how responsive they are..

Questions concering effort

1. How much effort are you willing to put into the course?
Explanation: This includes how much effort it requires to prepare a lecture, the after work and how much time is needed to solve the exercises.
